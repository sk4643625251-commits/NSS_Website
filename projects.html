About Our Projects

The National Service Scheme (NSS) volunteers at NIT Kurukshetra are dedicated to community service and social upliftment. Our project culture fosters responsibility, environmental consciousness, and sustainable growth.

2025 Milestone

Beginning of Project Culture

2025 marks a pivotal shift. Moving beyond traditional volunteering, we focus on structured, engineering solutions that provide long-term benefits to society.

It empowers volunteers to take ownership of impact-driven initiatives, creating a culture of continuous real-world change.

FLAGSHIP PROJECTS

Ongoing 2025 – Present

🌍 Campus Sustainability

Promoting self-reliant and environmentally responsible ecosystems within and beyond the campus.

Focus Areas:
  • Waste & Recycling
  • Water Harvesting
  • Green Infra
  • Developing a replicable model.
✅ Completed 2025

🌸 Women Empowerment

Building confidence, independence, and vocational skills through faculty-guided mentorship.

Key Impact:
  • Self-Defense Training
  • Vocational Skill Development
  • Mental Well-being Sessions

SUB-PROJECTS

RE:NIT

Recycling campus waste into usable components and artistic installations.

PAYJAL

Rainwater harvesting and ensuring pure drinking water across hostels.

Cooling Systems

Energy-efficient mechanisms to reduce carbon footprint in academic blocks.

Green Campus

Massive plantation drives to enhance air purity and biodiversity on campus.

Sustainable Dining

Optimizing mess consumption to eliminate food waste and using biodegradable materials.

Future Outlook

NSS NIT Kurukshetra envisions scaling these projects into city-wide replicable models, nurturing engineers who prioritize planetary health alongside technological advancement.
