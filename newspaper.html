Our Impact in Media

A glimpse of our community initiatives as featured in leading regional newspapers.

Vasant Panchami

Honoring Heritage: Vasant Panchami Celebrations at NSS

The auspicious occasion of Vasant Panchami was marked by the NSS unit with traditional ceremonies, celebrating the arrival of spring and seeking blessings for knowledge and wisdom.

NSS Day

NSS Day: Celebrating Decades of Service and Youth Leadership

Commemorating the foundation of the National Service Scheme, our volunteers engaged in various community service projects, reaffirming their commitment to 'Not Me, But You'.

Nasha Mukti

Campus Mobilization for a Drug-Free Society

In a powerful stand against substance abuse, the NSS unit organized workshops and awareness drives to highlight the socio-economic impact of addiction on youth.

Sewa Parv

Sewa Parv: Celebrating the Spirit of Selfless Service

NSS volunteers visited local welfare homes, emphasizing compassion and community bonding through direct service and interaction with the disadvantaged.

Shramdan

Shramdan: Volunteer-Led Cleanliness and Infrastructure Drive

Demonstrating the dignity of labor, NSS volunteers participated in a massive Shramdan drive to improve local campus facilities and promote hygiene.

Guru Nanak Ji

Life and Teachings of Guru Nanak Dev Ji

NSS volunteers highlighted the life and teachings of Guru Nanak Dev Ji, emphasizing equality, selfless service, and devotion to humanity as guiding principles for a better society.

Black Day

Black Day: Remembering Sacrifice and Raising Awareness

NSS volunteers observed Black Day to honor the sacrifices of martyrs and spread awareness about national integrity, unity, and the importance of standing against injustice.

Special Camp

7 Day Residential Camp Concludes at Dayalpur Village

A week-long special camp focused on rural development, awareness, and community service provided volunteers with immersive social service experiences.
