NSS 7-Day Camp 2026 – Dayalpur Village

Our NSS unit organized a 7-day camp in Dayalpur village focusing on social service, cleanliness drives, awareness campaigns, and community engagement.

Day 1 – Tree Plantation

Volunteers actively participated in tree plantation drives to promote environmental sustainability and create awareness about the importance of greenery.

Day 2 – Social Activities

Various social service activities were conducted to engage with the local community and understand their needs.

Day 3 – Sports Activities

Sports events were organized to encourage teamwork, physical fitness, and coordination among volunteers and villagers.

Day 4 – Swachhta Abhiyan

A cleanliness drive was carried out to spread awareness about hygiene and sanitation in the village.

Day 5 – Holi Celebration

Volunteers celebrated Holi with the villagers, promoting unity, joy, and cultural bonding.

Day 6 – Awareness & Counseling

Sessions on drug awareness, career counseling, and balanced diet were conducted to educate and empower the community.

Day 7 – Valedictory Ceremony

The camp concluded with a valedictory ceremony highlighting achievements, experiences, and volunteer participation.
