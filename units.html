Our NSS Units

Dedicated to the principles of social responsibility and community development through our six progressive service units. Each unit represents a unique dimension of service, growth, and positive change.

Nirbhaya

Courage Over Fear
1st Year

Nirbhaya introduces first-year students to social service through meaningful community engagement. Volunteers build sensitivity toward societal issues while instilling discipline and teamwork. Learn the profound value of consistent action and genuine service to the community.
