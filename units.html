Our NSS Units

Dedicated to the principles of social responsibility and community development through our six progressive service units.

NIRBHAYA

1st Year

Courage over fear. Serve with pure dedication.

Nirbhaya introduces first-year students to social service through basic community interaction, awareness drives, and campus cleanliness initiatives. The focus is on building sensitivity toward societal issues and instilling discipline and teamwork. Volunteers learn the value of small efforts and consistent actions in making a meaningful difference in society.
