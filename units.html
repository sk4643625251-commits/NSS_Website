NSS Service Units

Dedicated to the principles of social responsibility and community development through our six progressive service units.

Prayas

1st Year

Prayas introduces first-year students to social service through basic community interaction, awareness drives, and campus cleanliness initiatives. The focus is on building sensitivity toward societal issues and instilling discipline and teamwork. Volunteers learn the value of small efforts and consistent actions in making a meaningful difference in society.

Sahyog

2nd Year

Sahyog emphasizes collaboration and coordination of 2nd year students. Volunteers engage in organizing events, conducting surveys, and executing awareness campaigns. This stage connects them with communities through education drives, plantation activities, and health awareness camps. It enhances communication and planning skills while nurturing empathy and responsibility.

Sanskar

3rd Year

Sanskar aims to instill strong moral values and ethical conduct among volunteers. Through cultural activities, discussions, and awareness sessions, it shapes their character, encouraging them to become responsible citizens with integrity and social consciousness.

Sahaj

3rd Year

Sahaj focuses on promoting inner peace and mental well-being. Volunteers engage in yoga, meditation, and mindfulness practices, learning to lead a balanced life. It emphasizes simplicity, harmony, and emotional strength.

Seva

3rd Year

Seva is centered around active volunteering and hands-on service. Volunteers teach underprivileged children, organize cleanliness drives, and conduct blood donation camps. This unit fosters compassion, leadership, and commitment to community service.

Nirbhay

4th Year

Nirbhay represents fearless leadership. Final-year volunteers lead and mentor juniors, manage events, and represent NSS. They guide projects and ensure continuity. It is the stage where confidence meets compassion.
