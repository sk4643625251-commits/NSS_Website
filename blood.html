Why It Matters

Every Drop Saves Lives

Blood cannot be manufactured — only generous humans can provide it.

Save Up to 3 Lives

A single blood donation can be separated into components and save up to three different lives.

Health Benefits

Regular donation stimulates new red blood cell production, keeping your blood fresh and circulation healthy.

Only 30 Minutes

The actual donation process takes less than 30 minutes and the blood replenishes naturally within weeks.

Community Impact

1 in 3 people will need blood in their lifetime. Be the reason someone's family stays together.

Eligibility

Can You Donate?

Check if you meet these basic criteria before registering as a donor.

Age 18 – 65 years Donors must be within the eligible age bracket.
Weight ≥ 45 kg Minimum body weight ensures donor safety.
Hemoglobin ≥ 12.5 g/dL Adequate hemoglobin levels are required for donation.
No recent illness or surgery Must be free from infections and recent surgical procedures.
Donation gap ≥ 3 months Required interval between consecutive whole blood donations.
Healthy & well-rested Should be in good general health and adequately hydrated.
