Camp Highlights

What We Accomplished

Seven days of immersive community service that left a lasting mark on Dayalpur Village.

Tree Plantation

200+ saplings planted across Dayalpur village to improve air quality and biodiversity.

Youth Counseling

Interactive sessions empowering village youth on education, health, and career opportunities.

Swachhata Abhiyan

Community cleaning drives at schools and public spaces with 50+ volunteers each day.

Blood Donation

Regular blood donation camps organized to ensure a steady supply of blood for the community.

Digital Literacy & Career Guidance

Teaching basic digital skills and providing career guidance to school children and adults in the village.

Cultural Events

Evening cultural performances celebrating holi with volunteers and vilagers, fostering community spirit and joy.

Our Mission

Why We Organize Camps?

Residential camps are the heart of NSS. They allow students to live among the villagers, understand their struggles, and apply technical knowledge to solve real-world rural problems.

  • Build empathy, leadership and community responsibility in students.
  • Provide direct, hands-on community service at the grassroots level.
  • Bridge the gap between technical education and social responsibility.
