About NSS at Our College

The National Service Scheme (NSS) at our college is a vibrant platform dedicated to instilling the values of social responsibility, empathy, and community engagement among students. Guided by the noble motto "Not Me But You," our NSS unit strives to develop the personality and character of youth through voluntary community service. We believe that true education extends beyond classrooms, and active participation in societal welfare is integral to shaping responsible citizens.

Our volunteers actively engage in diverse initiatives aimed at uplifting the underprivileged and fostering sustainable development. From organizing regular blood donation camps and extensive cleanliness drives to promoting environmental awareness through tree plantation campaigns, we work consistently to address pressing social issues. Our efforts also extend to rural development and health awareness programs, ensuring a meaningful and lasting impact on the community.

By bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world challenges, NSS offers an enriching experience that nurtures leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving skills. Whether it is a local awareness campaign or a national integration effort, our volunteers serve with dedication, upholding the spirit of selfless service and driving positive change in society.

NSS Objectives

  • Community Understanding: To understand the community in which we work and identify its core needs and problems.
  • Social Responsibility: To develop among ourselves a sense of social and civic responsibility.
  • Practical Problem-Solving: To utilize our knowledge in finding practical solutions to individual and community problems.
  • Leadership & Teamwork: To acquire leadership qualities, foster democratic attitudes, and build strong teamwork capacity.
  • National Integration: To practice national integration and social harmony while striving for holistic community development.
