About NSS at NIT Kurukshetra

The National Service Scheme (NSS) at our college is a vibrant platform dedicated to instilling the values of social responsibility, empathy, and community engagement among students. Guided by the noble motto "Not Me But You," our NSS unit strives to develop the personality and character of youth through voluntary community service.

Our volunteers actively engage in diverse initiatives aimed at uplifting the underprivileged and fostering sustainable development. From organizing regular blood donation camps and extensive cleanliness drives to promoting environmental awareness through tree plantation campaigns, we work consistently to address pressing social issues.

By bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world challenges, NSS offers an enriching experience that nurtures leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving skills.

NSS Objectives

  • Community Understanding: Identify core needs and problems of the community.
  • Social Responsibility: Develop a sense of civic duty among youth.
  • Problem-Solving: Utilize knowledge for practical solutions.
  • Leadership: Foster democratic attitudes and teamwork.
  • Integration: Practice social harmony and national unity.

Our Identity

Who Are We?

The National Service Scheme (NSS) unit at NIT Kurukshetra is a passionate community of students dedicated to uplifting society through voluntary service. United by the motto "Not Me But You," we bridge the gap between technical education and real-world social challenges — one initiative at a time.

500+ Active Volunteers A diverse, motivated team of B.Tech and M.Tech students across disciplines.
Student-Led, Faculty-Guided Driven by student leadership under the mentorship of dedicated NSS faculty advisors.
Award-Winning Unit Recognised at regional and national levels for outstanding community service contribution.
Community First Philosophy Every action prioritises the well-being of local communities and the underprivileged.

What We Do?

From blood drives to village camps — we act wherever society needs us most.

Blood Donation

Village Development

NSS Annual Camp

Week-long residential camps in adopted villages, delivering hands-on social service and leadership.

Awareness Campaigns

Spreading awareness on hygiene, women empowerment, digital literacy and environmental conservation.
