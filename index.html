Words from Our Leaders

Guidance and inspiration from our esteemed directors and deans.

Prof. Brahmjit Singh

Officiating Director | NIT Kurukshetra

Welcome to the National Service Scheme (NSS). Our aim is to develop the personality of students through community service. NSS provides a platform for young individuals to contribute to society and build leadership skills. We encourage all volunteers to actively participate in social initiatives like cleanliness drives, blood donation camps, and awareness programs. Together, we can create a positive impact in society.

Dr. Lillie Dewan

Dean Student Welfare | NIT Kurukshetra

National Service Scheme , also known as NSS has a philosophy “Not Me But You”, is an extension of activities for students pursuing higher education to increase their participation in community service, in addition to their curriculum. Students should understand the community in which they work, their relations to that community, and develop a strong sense of responsibility and sacrifice towards the betterment of society. Students should understand that NSS is a programme that aims the development of each volunteer’s personality through community service with no expectation of reward. अफालकाङ्क्षिभिर्यज्ञो विधिध्र्ष्टो य इज्यते I यष्टव्यमेवेति मनः समाधाय स सात्त्विकः II 17.11I Aphalakanksibhir yajno vidhi-drshto ya ijyate Yashtavyam eveti manah samadhaya sa sattvikah “The smallest act of kindness is worth more than the grandest intention." – Oscar Wilde
